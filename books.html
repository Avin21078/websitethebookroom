Classics :

And Then There Were None By Agatha Christie

#Fiction #Mystery #Classics #Crime

Ten strangers are lured to a secluded island and mysteriously killed one by one.

Why it's a good read: A masterclass in suspense and plot twists, widely considered Christie's best.

Suitable for: Mystery lovers and readers who enjoy tense, cleverly constructed whodunits.

Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen

#Fiction #Romance #Classics

Summary : A witty romantic novel about Elizabeth Bennet navigating love, class, and first impressions in Regency England.

Why it's a good read: Clever dialogue, sharp social commentary, and timeless romance.

Suitable for : Readers who enjoy classic literature, romance, and strong, independent heroines.

The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald

#Fiction #Mystery #Classics #Crime

Summary : A tragic tale of wealth, love, and the American Dream set in the Roaring Twenties.

Why it's a good read: Evocative writing, vivid symbolism, and a powerful look at ambition and loss.

Suitable for : Fans of historical fiction, themes of ambition, and bittersweet endings.

Jane Eyre by Charlotte Brontë

#Fiction #Dystopia #Classics #Politics

Summary : A gothic romance following an orphan's journey toward independence, love, and moral integrity.

Why it's a good read: Combines mystery, romance, and a strong female lead with emotional depth.

Suitable for : Lovers of historical romance, gothic settings, and character-driven stories.

Animal Farm by George Orwell

#Fiction #Mystery #Classics #Crime

Summary : A group of farm animals stage a revolution to overthrow their human farmer, Mr. Jones, hoping to create a society where all animals are equal.

Why it's a good read: Sharp satire on power, corruption, and revolution.

Suitable for : Readers interested in politics, and short impactful reads.

Crime and Punishment by Fyodor Dostoevsk

#Fiction #Classics #Philosophy #Crime

Summary : A psychological drama about a young man who commits murder and wrestles with guilt and morality.

Why it's a good read: Deep exploration of conscience, justice, and redemption.

Suitable for : Readers who enjoy complex characters and philosophical themes.

Non-Fiction :

The Diary of a Young Girl By Anne Frank

#Nonfiction #History #Classics #Memoir

Summary : The real-life diary of a Jewish girl hiding from the Nazis during WWII.

Why it's a good read: Moving, honest, and inspiring insight into hope under unimaginable hardship.

Suitable for : Everyone, especially those interested in history and human resilience.

Atomic Habits By James Clear

#Nonfiction #SelfHelp #Psychology

A practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones through small, consistent changes.

Why it's a good read: Actionable advice backed by psychology, easy to apply to everyday life.

Suitable for : Anyone interested in self-improvement, productivity, and lasting change.

Educated by Tara Westover

#Fiction #Memoir #Biography

Summary : A memoir of a woman who grows up in a survivalist family with no formal schooling and pursues higher education.

Why it's a good read: An inspiring story about self-discovery, resilience, and the power of learning.

Suitable for : Fans of memoirs, personal growth, and overcoming adversity.

Thinking, Fast and Slow by Daniel Kahneman

#Nonfiction #Psychology #Self Help

An exploration of how our minds work, with two thinking systems fast, intuitive and slow, deliberate.

Why it's a good read: Eye-opening insights into decision-making and human behavior.

Suitable for : Readers interested in psychology, economics, and self-improvement.

Mystery :

The Da Vinci Code By Dan Brown

#Mystery #Thriller #Suspense

Summary : A fast-paced thriller uncovering hidden codes and secrets within art and history.

Why it's a good read: Combines mystery, history, and relentless suspense.

Suitable for : Fans of page-turning thrillers and historical puzzles.

And Then There Were None By Agatha Christie

#Fiction #Mystery #Classics #Crime

Summary : Ten strangers are invited to a remote island, only to be killed off one by one.

Why it's a good read: Masterfully plotted murder mystery with an unforgettable twist.

Suitable for : Mystery lovers and fans of clever, tense storytelling.

Girl On The Train By Paula Hawkins

#Fiction #Mystery #Thriller #Crime

A psychological thriller about an unreliable witness entangled in a missing person case.

Why it's a good read: Twisty, tense, and full of shifting perspectives.

Suitable for : Fans of suspense and unreliable narrators.

The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes By Sir Arthur Conan Doyle

#Fiction #Mystery #Classics #Crime

Summary : A collection of detective stories featuring the brilliant Sherlock Holmes.

Why it's a good read: Clever mysteries, sharp deduction, and timeless characters.

Suitable for : Mystery fans of all ages.

A Good Girl's Guide To Murder By Holly Jackson

#Fiction #Mystery #Crime

Summary : A high school student investigates a closed murder case for her school project—and uncovers dangerous truths.

Why it's a good read: Fast-paced, modern mystery with strong twists.

Suitable for : Young adult readers and fans of crime thrillers.
