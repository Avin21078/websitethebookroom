Welcome to the Reading Room!

The Reading Room is a space built to inspire a love of reading for everyone, no matter your age, background, or reading ability.
We understand that finding time, choosing the right book, or even staying focused can make reading feel like a challenge.
The Reading Room is here to guide, support, and inspire you making reading not just easier, but something you look forward to every day.

Recommended Books :

Fault In Our Stars By John Green

#Fiction #Romance #YoungAdult

The Fault in Our Stars is a touching novel about two teens with cancer, Hazel and Augustus, who fall in love and confront life, death, and meaning together. It’s recommended for its emotional depth, relatable characters, and thoughtful insights on love and loss.

Atomic Habits By James Clear

#Nonfiction #SelfHelp #Psychology

Atomic Habits by James Clear is a practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones through small, consistent changes. It’s recommended for its clear strategies, real-life examples, and powerful insights on how tiny habits lead to big results over time.

And Then There Were None By Agatha Christie

#Fiction #Mystery #Classics #Crime

And Then There Were None by Agatha Christie is a suspenseful mystery where ten strangers are invited to a remote island and are killed off one by one. It’s recommended for its clever plot, intense atmosphere, and shocking twist ending that keeps readers guessing.
