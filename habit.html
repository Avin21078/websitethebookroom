Why Build a Reading Habit?

Focus and concentration.
Boosts vocabulary and imagination.
Reduces stress and helps you relax.
Gives you a healthy break from screens.

How to Start :

Set a Small. Achievable Goal. Start with just 5 to 10 minutes a day.
Pick the Right Book. Choose something you're genuinely excited about, not just what's “popular.”
Make It Part of Your Routine, Tie reading to an existing habit (e.g. after breakfast, before bed).

Common Obstacles & How to Overcome Them :

“I'm too busy.” → Break reading into small chunks; 5 minutes is better than none.
“I lose interest.” → Don't force yourself to finish every book — it's okay to DNF (Did Not Finish).
“I get distracted.” → Find a quiet spot and put your phone in another room.

Staying Consistent

Track your progress in a journal or app.
Join a reading challenge or book club.
Reward yourself for milestones (finished a book? Treat yourself!).
Share what you're reading with friends to stay motivated.
